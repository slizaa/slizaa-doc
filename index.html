What is slizaa?

Slizaa is an interactive and customizable software architecture workbench, that allows you to explore and visualize the structure and the dependencies of your software system.

Slizaa uses Neo4j/jQAssistant and provides a front end with a bunch of specific tools and viewers to provide an easy-to-use in-depth insight of your software's architecture.
